About New Vision Enterprise

New Vision Enterprise exists to help men transform their lives by developing a new vision for who they are, embracing their Second Wind, and working harder on themselves than they do on their circumstances.

About

I've experienced success, setbacks, victories, disappointments, and seasons that forced me to start over.

I've led teams in corporate America, built businesses, written books, and spoken to audiences across different stages of life. But some of life's greatest lessons didn't come from a boardroom, a business, or a stage.

They came through life itself.
Through marriage.
Through fatherhood.
Through responsibility.
Through learning that growth is a lifelong process.

Becoming a father later in life reinforced something I have come to believe deeply: your future is not determined by where you start, but by who you become.

Those experiences shaped the message behind New Vision Enterprise, the WHOY Framework, and Second Wind.

Today, I remain committed to helping people expand their vision, develop their potential, and become who they were created to be.

Because life gets better when you get better.

Mission Statement

New Vision Enterprise is a personal development company that helps people improve their lives by improving themselves.

Through speaking, books, coaching, and practical development frameworks, we teach leadership, responsibility, personal growth, and purpose. Our belief is simple: life gets better when you get better.

New Vision is the company. Second Wind is the message. WHOY (Work Harder On Yourself) is the methodology.

Vision

"To create a generation of men who lead with responsibility, live with purpose, and never stop developing themselves."

Our Framework

New Vision is the company. Second Wind is the message. WHOY is the methodology. Together, they help men move from where they are to who they are capable of becoming.
