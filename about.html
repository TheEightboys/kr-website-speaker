About

About New Vision Enterprise

New Vision Enterprise exists to help men become the version of themselves their purpose requires. We believe life gets better when you get better. Through speaking, books, coaching, and digital content, we help men develop the discipline, responsibility, and structure needed to lead themselves, their families, and their future with intention. This is not motivation. This is transformation through personal development.

The Founder

KR Henderson is an author, speaker, business owner, and creator of the WHOY philosophy—Work Harder On Yourself. After decades of leadership in corporate America, entrepreneurship, marriage, fatherhood, setbacks, and reinvention, KR discovered a simple truth: Most men work harder on their circumstances than they do on themselves. That realization became WHOY. Today, through books, courses, speaking engagements, and the Second Wind platform, KR helps men rebuild confidence, clarity, discipline, and purpose.

Mission

"To help men transform their lives by working harder on themselves than they do on their circumstances."

Vision

"To create a generation of men who lead with responsibility, live with purpose, and never stop developing themselves."

Our Framework

New Vision is the company. Second Wind is the message. WHOY is the methodology. Together, they help men move from where they are to who they are capable of becoming.
