Life Gets Better When You Get Better

KR Henderson is the Founder and Chief Development Officer of New Vision Enterprise, an educational and personal development company dedicated to helping people expand their vision, develop their potential, and become who they were created to be.

Through speaking, training, coaching, books, the Second Wind podcast, and the WHOY (Work Harder On Yourself) Framework, KR provides practical principles for leadership, personal growth, responsibility, and purpose.

Drawing from decades of experience in corporate leadership, entrepreneurship, and personal development, KR equips individuals and organizations with the tools to grow, lead, and reach their full potential.

Whether you're rebuilding, reinventing, or stepping into a new season of life, growth begins with a simple truth:
The greatest investment you will ever make is in yourself.
