Second Wind

A New Season. A New Perspective. A New Opportunity.

Second Wind is for men who find themselves carrying more responsibility, discovering a newfound purpose, facing unexpected challenges, or stepping into a season of life they never saw coming.

Some men become fathers later.
Some start over after loss, disappointment, or setbacks.
Some discover purpose after years of feeling stuck.
Others simply realize that the life they want requires a better version of themselves.

Second Wind is not about starting over.
It's about finding assignment after you thought you were done.
It's about moving forward with greater wisdom, greater clarity, and greater purpose.

Through podcasts, resources, conversations, and community, Second Wind helps men navigate life's transitions, embrace new opportunities, and move forward without feeling late—but assigned.

Because your best years may not be behind you.
They may be waiting for you in your Second Wind.
